Kim Jennie

Kim Jennie (Jennie Kim) Jennie is a South Korean singer, rapper, and fashion icon best known as a member of the K-pop group BLACKPINK. She debuted as a solo artist in 2018 with “SOLO” and is also recognized globally for her influence in fashion and brand ambassadorships.

Travis Scott

Travis Scott Travis Scott is an American rapper, singer, and record producer known for his energetic performances and atmospheric trap sound. He gained major success with albums like Astroworld and is one of the most influential figures in modern hip-hop culture.

The Weeknd

The Weeknd (Abel Tesfaye) The Weeknd is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and producer famous for his distinctive voice and dark, emotional R&B style. With hit albums like After Hours and Starboy, he has become one of the world’s best-selling music artists.
